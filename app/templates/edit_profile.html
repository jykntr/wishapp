Avatar Information

Avatars are provided by Robohash and Gravatar.

If you haven't configured an avatar on the Gravatar website a default Robohash avatar will be created for you. Robohash generates an avatar based on an MD5 hash computed from your email address. Your real email address isn't used to keep it private. If you change your email address your avatar will also change.

To use an avatar of your choice, create an account on Gravatar and configure an avatar. When you come back to this website you will see it being used automatically.

For more information on how to setup a Gravatar account click here. It is easiest if you use the same email address as you used for this account, but it is possible to configure additional email addresses for your Gravatar account.
